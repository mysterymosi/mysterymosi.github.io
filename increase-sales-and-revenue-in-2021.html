What is Oja Srores?

As a CEO or an entrepreneur of a store, I’m sure you’ve considered putting your products online to sell or this is a process you have taken already and still haven’t gotten your desired sales increase. That is proof that how and what platform you use to showcase your products matters.

Oja Stores is an e-commerce grocery store. Simply put it’s a place where you can shop online and get access to buy fresh groceries in Nigeria from any Supermarket of your choice. As a store owner you must have started seeing the opportunity buried in this - there’s more.

Oja stores plays the role of a third party online market platform for supermarket to get easier access to their customer and making the customer’s online shopping experience to the next level.

Oja helps create a hassle free shopping experience for customer by elevating and preserving the hospitality experience across the entire payment, checkout and delivery experience using menu design, commissary techniques and process automation applied to the latest technology.
