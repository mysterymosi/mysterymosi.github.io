How to Make Fried Rice – New fast and Delicious Recipe

Hey, welcome back to our little food corner. We all know that there are multiple fried rice recipes and a lot of home recipes which we have all grown up with around our homes. But if you are looking for an easier recipe or an easy to implement recipe we’ve got you. Here is a simple motherly recipe that you could easy implement in your home.

Recipe Ingredients Needed

  1. Butter or Oil (preferably vegetable or sunflower).
  2. Rice
  3. Mixed vegetable
  4. Green peas
  5. Sweet Corn
  6. Shrimps, liver or sausage (optional)
  7. Sausage (optional)
  8. Spring onions
  9. Mixed peppers
  10. Seasoning of choice

How to Prepare Fried Rice: With New Recipe

  • First start by cleaning and parboiling your rice accordingly depending on the rice you are using. Some rice take longer to parboil than some so pay attention to the type of rice you use.
  • While your rice is being parboiled you can wash your vegetables and start cutting them. Be sure to wash them with salt (PS hygiene first)
  • If you would be using shrimps or sausages you can prep them and season them at this stage. You would also need to fry the shrimp to give them a stronger texture and to avoid undercooked shrimps.

  • The next thing you would want to do is fry your vegetables for about 5 minutes before adding your cooked seasoned rice and frying them all together and closing it to cook for another 5 minutes.
  • And there we have it your fried rice is ready to be eaten.

Fried Rice Seasonings

  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • Fried rice seasoning
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Curry
  • Chicken stock (if available)
